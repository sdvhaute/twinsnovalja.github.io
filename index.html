Welcome to

Twins Novalja

Luxury residences at the heart of Pag

Discover peace, comfort and quiet at the heart of beautiful Novalja with a stay at our Twins residences. Freshly renovated in 2022, the Twins houses facilitate all the Croatian sun has to offer from dusk till dawn. Get carried away by the rhythm of the tides and enjoy the Dalmatians like never before!
The Twins houses are located on the island of Pag, at the sea town of Novalja. Part of the numerous Dalmatian islands of the Croatian cost, Pag - known best for its Paski Sir cheese and its millenium old olive trees - breaths history and culture.
Unlike the vast remoteness of the rest of the island, Novalja is a bustling little town the whole year round with a beautiful promenade by the sea, offering excellent dining options at sunset. After closing hours, the city center provides fun and enjoyment to young and old, wanting to spend the night out at Zrce Beach.
At the end of a crossroad on a quiet street (Punta Mira = Peace Point), our residences grant proximity to all the necessary amenities of a comfortable stay, without compromising on privacy and peace. Therefore, Twins Novalja really embodies the idea of offering you the best of both worlds: ease and calm!
